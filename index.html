About BIS

The Barangay Information System (BIS) is a full-stack solution designed to modernize local governance. It simplifies resident management, service requests, household tracking, business records, and official documents, while providing real-time dashboards and exportable reports.

Core Modules

Residents

Manage resident profiles, households, and demographics.

Service Requests

Track and process barangay services and clearances.

Transactions

Generate certificates and documents with audit trail.

Households

Organize residents into households with relationships.

Businesses

Register and manage barangay-level businesses.

Dashboard

Visualize key statistics with interactive charts.

Technology Stack

Frontend

Backend

Database & Cloud
